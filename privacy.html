Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

This policy explains what data is processed in LiftLog 5x5 Pro, where it is stored, and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Workout logs, set/rep/weight data, notes, and timing data.
  • User-entered fitness data such as body measurements and body-fat values.
  • App settings and in-app preference data.
  • When Sign in with Apple is used: account identifiers (for example, Apple user ID and email if shared).

2. Data Storage and Infrastructure

Data may be stored in Firebase infrastructure (for example, Authentication and database services) for account-based synchronization. Some data may also be stored locally on your device.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • Providing workout tracking, progress analysis, and core app features.
  • Account authentication and cross-device sync.
  • Notification features (workout and rest reminders).

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase services for data storage and authentication. Sign in with Apple may be used for authentication. No advertising network is used in the app.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied for data security. However, absolute security cannot be guaranteed for internet transmission or cloud systems.

6. User Rights

You can edit/delete your data within the app or submit data requests via the support email address.
